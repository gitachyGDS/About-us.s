About Us

Shree Secondary School Tulsipur Dang

Secondary School Tulsipur Center School is a leading educational institution in Tulsipur, offering a diverse range of CTEVT-approved courses such as Diploma in Information Technology (DIT), Diploma in Geomatices Engineering (DGeo), Diploma in Electrical Engineering, and Diploma in Business Management. Our school is committed to providing top-notch services, including experienced faculty, modern facilities like well-equipped classrooms and labs, a resourceful library, sports facilities, and counseling services. We aim to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in their chosen fields, while also nurturing a passion for lifelong learning and social responsibility. At Secondary School Tulsipur Center School, we strive to create an environment where excellence is fostered, opportunities abound, and students are prepared to excel in their academic and professional endeavors.
